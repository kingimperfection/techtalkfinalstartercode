The Birth Of a Legend

CHance the rappers story is not an uncommon one,Having considered a career in music throughout his high school years, Chancelor Bennett, aka Chance the Rapper, used a ten-day suspension during his senior year to record his debut mixtape, 10 Day. Uploaded immediately to the Internet in 2012, the tape grabbed the attention of Forbes magazine, which featured it in the publication's Cheap Tunes column, as well as Childish Gambino, who asked Chance to join his 2012 tour of America as opening act.
